# Multi-Perspective Analysis: Lunar PNT: The Battle for the Prime Meridian of the Moon

## Executive Summary
The lunar PNT standardization battle represents a critical inflection point where technical standards become geopolitical weapons. Red Hat intuition strongly warns this is about much more than navigation—it's about who controls the fundamental infrastructure of the lunar economy. Most promising creative insight: applying Internet governance models could create a "Lunar ICANN" that prevents any single nation from controlling lunar coordinate systems while maintaining technical coherence.

**What We Know:**
